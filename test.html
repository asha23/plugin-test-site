DuVall's School of Cosmetology

DuVall’s School of Cosmetology is the cosmetology school of choice for many not only because it conveniently located in the Mid-Cities and priced competitively, but for many more reasons.

Success

DuVall’s is committed to your success and future employment; we work with each student individually to ensure they are not only ready to pass the State Board Exam but start a successful career in the beauty industry.

Nationally Recognized

DuVall’s was recently ranked first NATIONWIDE for our skincare (esthetics) program. This determination was made by Washington Monthly. Read more about it on this page which includes links to the rankings and methodology.

Fun

DuVall School is just fun. The staff and students work great together to create a fun and interesting environment to do their work and learning. Working and studying are so much easier when you are in an upbeat environment and with a community of people having fun together.

Professional

DuVall’s believes you learn from example. As soon as you walk into DuVall School of Cosmetology you notice the professional environment. The facility is clean, with an open floorplan and with professional and friendly staff. Students and staff alike feel good when they show up to work and learn.

Job Placement

Duvall’s has been serving Fort Worth and Tarrant County since 1983. They have great connections in the area and a great success with helping the graduates get the job they want. Duvall’s great reputation puts their graduates a step ahead of the rest.

Financial Assistance

The Financial Aid Department at DuVall’s consists of knowledgeable, qualified staff to work with you throughout the enrollment process to develop your personalized financial plan. DuVall’s staff can educate you whether it is a grant, direct loan, scholarship, etc.
